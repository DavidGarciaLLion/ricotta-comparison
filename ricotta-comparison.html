Overview

Not all ricottas are created equal

From Galbani's decadent double cream to Sargento's surprisingly lean fat-free option, the caloric spread across brands is significant — nearly 3× difference between the highest and lowest. Sugar stays fairly consistent (it's naturally-occurring lactose, not added sugar), while fat is the biggest variable.
